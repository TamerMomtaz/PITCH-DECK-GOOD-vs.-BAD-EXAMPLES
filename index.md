## ğŸ¯ THE KEY DIFFERENCES

### **Bad Slides:**
- Lots of complete sentences
- Paragraphs of text
- Presenter reads from screen
- Audience zones out
- No visuals
- Boring
- Too much detail

### **Good Slides:**
- Big headlines
- 3-5 bullet points MAX
- Strong visuals
- Presenter tells story
- Audience stays engaged
- Memorable
- Just enough detail
